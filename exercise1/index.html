How to prepare for an Earthquake

An earthquake is a very destructive natural diaster; particularly in the Pacific Rim region. After an earthquake, your home may be a mess and you might be left without a water supply and energy. There are several things you can do to prepare for an earthquake before it happens, to minimize the damages and potential for injury in and around your home.
